Video Games

Currently Playing:

Slay The Spire is a card based rougelike, where the map and your deck changes each run. There are 4 character to play as, each with a different card type and mechanic.

I have always loved Minecraft, and still do. Being creative and building houses is very fun, and you can mod it if vanilla is to basic for you!

Favourite games of all time:


Outer Wilds is an incredible game, best played completely blind. It rewards curiosity, and the DLC adds to the game perfectly

A Hat in Time is one of the first PC games I ever played. It's an adorable 3D platformer with memorable characters and fun mechanics!



Music



Currently I have the Wandersong OST by A Shell In The Pit on repeat. It's some great music, and fits my vibe. My favourite track is no.97 'Even the Tiniest Bugs'.

I love the Neon Green Chambers albums by Vandoorea. It's a 5 album series of Portal OST remixes, and I frequently find my self returning to it. My favourite track is from Chapter II, track no.3 'Yesterday I Saw a Deer'.



The Rhythm Doctor soundtrack is full of lots of different genres, that fit each level in the game perfectly. My favourite track is either 'Super Battleworn Insomniac' or 'Dreams Don't Stop'.

This soundtrack by C418 (the original minecraft composer) is beautiful and weirdly nostalgic to me. It has a strange vibe that I can't help but love. My favourite track is 'the weirdest year of your life'.
